Overview

Sales Performance Report

Executive Findings

Sales have a positively skewed distribution as the top 25% observations account for about 57% of the total

The values for sales range from a minimum of 10 and all the way up to a maximum of 2,754. The total sales amount to 126,449 with the average sales per observation being 253. (This Section comes only if trend is missing)

Sales had a phenomenal growth during Jan-Dec, primarily driven huge number of high-value observations

Sales witnessed strong growth (232%) over the last 12 months and the total sales amounts to $126K with an average of $270 per observation. The distribution seems to be positively skewed as the top 25% observations account for about 57% of the total sales.

Despite the robust overall growth, the sales figures are likely to experience a moderate decline

The total sales grew from $4,406 in Jan 2014 to $14,628 in Dec 2014, but the contribution from a streak of 3 months (Aug-Oct) amounts to a total of $651,037, which is almost half of the overall sales for the 12-month period. Despite the strong growth, sales are likely to decline by 9% over the next 6 months.

City and Marketing Channel have strong influence on Sales as they explain significant portion of variance in sales.

Cities – Chicago, Los Angeles, Washington, and San Diego – are not performing well both in terms of sales contribution and growth. Also, Direct and Email are the marketing channels that don’t grow sales nor have significant contribution to current sales.

mAdvisor has observed opportunities for improving the sales figures by engaging with other key factor and metrics.

  • Focusing more on cities, Miami, Seattle, and Boston would enhance overall sales, as they show high potential for growth and hold significant portion of current sales.
  • A one percentage increase in marketing cost for the segment, where Deal Type is Gourmet, Discount Range is 21 to 30 percent, and Marketing Channel is Google Ads, will correspond to 0.7% increase in sales.
  • The marginal effect of Shipping cost on sales is very significant on Price Range, 101 to 500, as sales move by almost 46 units for every unit decrease in marketing cost. (Skipping)
  • High-value segment (top quartile sales observations) has an average sale of $613, which is 124% higher than the overall average ($270). Here are the typical characteristics of high-value sales observations.
    • Discount Range is 31 to 40, Product Group is Electronics, and Price Range is101 to 500
    • Discount Range is 31 to 40, Price Range is 101 to 500, and Shipping Cost is less than 1.8
    • Price Range is 50 to 100 and City is Miami
